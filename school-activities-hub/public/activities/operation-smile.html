Operation Smile

Volunteer-led charity supporting cleft surgeries and awareness.

Operation Smile is a student-led volunteering and charity club focused on raising awareness and funds for children around the world who need cleft lip and palate surgeries. Members organize campaigns, coordinate donation drives, and partner with local organizations to make an impact.

Meeting Times: Tuesdays, 3:30–4:30 PM

Location: Room 201 (Community Service Center)

Contact: Ms. Alvarez — alvarez@school.edu
